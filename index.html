Chef Maria

I don’t remember the first time I tied on an apron, but my memories are full of kitchen smells, long tables, fresh pasta, and the joy of sharing food.

Chef Maria brings authentic Italian cooking to homes and private events across South Florida. Her cuisine is warm, elegant, and rooted in the flavors of Verona and Northern Italy.

More than a meal, Chef Maria’s table is a true place for food, wine, and connection.

Every dish is prepared with care, tradition, and the feeling of being welcomed home.

After years of cooking, studying, and preparing Italian food for families and events, Chef Maria creates experiences that feel personal, generous, and memorable.

My Philosophy

Every recipe is a testament to heritage, family, and tradition.

By keeping these traditions alive, we allow them to adapt and become part of modern life.

Chef Maria’s passion for cooking comes from the connection between the past and the present: handmade pasta, slow sauces, seasonal ingredients, and flavors that bring people together.

Sitting around a table is not only about eating. It is a way to share stories, celebrate family, and create lasting memories.

Cooking is a means of genuine exchange.

From the preparation of ingredients to the last toast, cooking is about sharing, hospitality, and feeling welcome.

When Chef Maria enters your kitchen, you are not a stranger. You are a guest invited to share a unique Italian experience.
